🛡️ How to stay safe

1
Never click links in unsolicited messages. Copy and paste the URL here before visiting any page you didn't expect.
2
Verify urgent requests by phone. Scammers create false urgency. Always verify through official channels.
3
Inspect the sender's email domain. "support@paypa1.com" is not the same as "support@paypal.com".
4
Legitimate companies never ask for passwords. If someone asks for your password or PIN, it's a scam.
